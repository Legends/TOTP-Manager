Run from repository root after publish.

PowerShell:
$env:SIGNING_CERT_BASE64 = "<base64-pfx-from-secret>"
$env:SIGNING_CERT_PASSWORD = "<pfx-password>"
./TOTP/Properties/Signing/Sign-App.ps1 -ExePath ./publish/TOTP.UI.WPF.exe

Alternative (local path outside repo):
$env:SIGNING_CERT_PATH = "C:\secure\codesign.pfx"
$env:SIGNING_CERT_PASSWORD = "<pfx-password>"
./TOTP/Properties/Signing/Sign-App.ps1 -ExePath ./publish/TOTP.UI.WPF.exe
